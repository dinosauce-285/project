Hufflepuff was one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Its founder
was the medieval witch Helga Hufflepuff. Hufflepuff was the most inclusive among the four houses;
valuing hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play rather than a particular aptitude in its
members. The emblematic animal was a badger, yellow and black were its house colours. The Head
of Hufflepuff was Pomona Sprout, and the Fat Friar was the House's patron ghost.
